What Does Shawnee Have?

Shawnee's menu is careful curartion of foods from all over the Philippines. With a focus of the family's home region of Abra, Shawnee also loves serving the Filipino classics!

Below, you will find dishes in the the following categories:

  • Mains
  • Classics
  • Desserts
  • Seafood
  • Vegetables

MAINS

Adobo (Pork/Chicken) • Igado (Pork) • Menudo (Pork) • Dinakdakan (Pork) • Steak (Pork/Beef) • Sinigang (Pork) • Lechon Kawali • Inihaw (Chicken/Pork) • Kaderela (Pork/Beef) • Papitan (Beef) • Kare Kare (Beef)

CLASSICS

Pancit (Bihon/Solanghon/Canton) • Fresh Lumpia • Longanisa (Ilokano) • Barbeque (Pork/Chicken) • Egg Roll (Pork) • Spaghetti • Tocino • Atsara • Embutido
